Welcome to the Student-Web Guidance!

Here are some frequently asked questions to help guide you through your academic struggles.

What is academic stress?

Academic stress refers to the emotional strain caused by workload, exams, or deadlines.

How to manage exam stress?

Stay organized, take regular breaks, practice relaxation, and sleep well.

Tips for balancing life?

Plan your schedule, prioritize tasks, and make time for relaxation and friends.

Where to seek help?

Reach out to guidance officers, school counselors, or a trusted support network.
